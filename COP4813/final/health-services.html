Health Services

UNF provides medical health insurance for its international students. If you do not have insurance you can apply for it via UNF. UNF also has a great free clinic for all its students.

Services available to UNF Students

The following services, which are funded by student health fees, are provided to students:

  • Diagnosis and treatment of illness, injury and emergencies on campus
  • Referrals for specialist care
  • Consultation for specific concerns

Services provided at a nominal cost to student include:

  • Physical exams
  • Minor surgical procedures
  • TB skin testing and immunizations
  • Allergy injections
  • Pregnancy testing
  • Laboratory services such as blood counts, throat cultures, urinalysis, cholesterol/lipid panel, and other specimens are collected on site and submitted to a local laboratory
  • Limited prescription medications
  • STD (sexually transmitted diseases) screening and treatment
  • Gynecological exams & birth control
  • X-Rays

The following services are available through local clinics or hospitals. Students are responsible for payment for these services.

  • X-rays
  • Emergency treatment
  • Hospitalization

Emergency Care

Three emergency rooms are 15 minutes driving time from the campus and are open 24 hours a day, 365 days a year. If you have an on-campus emergency, dial 911.
