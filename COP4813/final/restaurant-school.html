Places to Eat on Campus



Osprey Cafe
Location: Building 16
Description: Osprey Cafe is UNFs resident dine in facility. It is a buffet style facility
with a many stations which each have a variety of choices of food for one to eat. They serve breakfast
lunch and dinner and they accept the UNF meal plan.

The Boathouse
Location: Student Union
Description: A UNF tradition; you will find yourself in a fun, up-beat atmosphere enjoying classic
American food, beverage and entertainment.

Quiznos Subs
Location: Student Union
Description: Sub shop that offers traditional subs, grilled flatbreads, salads and wraps, sub sliders, soups,
and more.

Coyote Jacks
Location: Student Union
Description: Burger restaurant that offers fire grilled burgers, chicken sandwiches, chicken tenders,
regular and sweet potato fries, and onion rings.

Chick-n-Grill
Location: Student Union
Foods: Restaurant that serves Rice Bowls, Wraps, Salads, Chicken Tenders, and more.

Papa Johns
Location: Student Union
Description: Pizza restaurant that provides a menu that's prepared with only the finest ingredients, serves
personal size pizzas only.

Outtakes
Location: Student Union, Alumni Square
Description: Upscale convenience market that offers a full line of grab & go gourmet sandwiches, soups,
salads, hot and cold beverages, groceries and baked goods. Both Outtakes locations feature daily
Au Bon Pain soups.

Jamba Juice
Location: Wellness complex
Foods: Smoothie shop that offers nutritious smoothies, California flatbreads, breakfast wraps, steel cut
oatmeal and yummy baked goods.

Chick-Fil-A
Location: Alumni Square
Foods: Restaurant that offers chicken sandwiches, chicken nuggets, salads, and chicken biscuits and tater
tots in the morning for breakfast.

Chop'd & Wrap'd
Location: Alumni Square
Description: Healthy option that offers fresh chopped salads and wraps, sushi, ice cream, milkshakes, and more.

Starbucks
Location: Alumni Square
Description: A great spot to study and get work done; serves high-quality whole bean coffees with fresh,
rich brewed, Italian-style espresso beverages, and assortment of pastries and confections.

Ozzie's Convenience Store & Grill
Location: Osprey Fountains
Description: Enjoy a made-to-order grille menu, breakfast all day and a convenience market offering
grab & go sandwiches, salads, hot and cold beverages, groceries and baked goods.
