Transportation



Transportation services There are several ways to commute when you are at UNF.
UNF provides a great free shuttle service to all students to get around the campus.
We also have shuttles that can take you to town center. For exploring Jacksonville
you can also take the public transport which has bus stops spread around campus.
if you have some money to spare you can even take a cab/taxi.

Bus Service

Osprey shuttle At UNF we have a number of osprey shuttles to take
your around the campus.there are many bus stops and the frequency of busses is
very 5-10 min.these shuttles are free for all unf students. We also have shuttles
taking you to towncenter but the frequency is about twice a day.

Public transport We also have a number of public transport bus stops spread across
campus. You can get a bus to towncenter , downtown or anywhere else you need to
go.the frequency of these busses is every 30-60 min.public transport costs between
2-5$. www.jtafla.com



Taxi Service

There are various taxi service providers in jacksonville.They are very
safe,punctual and efficient. You can get a taxi by calling the taxi service company
or if you are using an app for their service.
