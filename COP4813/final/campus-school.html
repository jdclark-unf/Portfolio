What is there to do on campus?



UNF Master Calendar of Events
UNF offers a master calendar that shows all of the upcoming events around campus.
UNF Calendar

Work Out
Students can go to the Student Wellness Complex which is located across the street from the
student union. The wellness complex is a gym that features state of the art equipment,a 1/8 mile
track,and a 32-foot rock climbing wall.

Sporting Events
UNF has many sport teams that have games and events all throughout the school year; with
sports such as basketball, baseball & softball, soccer, tennis, and track & field. Students get
into all sporting events for free with their student id.
UNF Athlethics

Intramural Sports
The Intramural Sports program provides students and faculty the opportunity to participate in
athletic and non-athletic recreational activities. Some of the sports student can participate in are
basketball, flag football, soccer, softball, and tennis.
